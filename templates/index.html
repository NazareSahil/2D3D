What is DICOM?

DICOM (Digital Imaging and Communications in Medicine) is a standard protocol for the management and transmission of medical images and related data and is used in many healthcare facilities.DICOM was originally developed by the National Electrical Manufacturers Association (NEMA) and the American College of Radiology (ACR).

It is a registered trademark of NEMA and is governed by the DICOM Standards Committee, a collaboration of users across all medical imaging specialties with an interest in the standardization of medical imaging information.
